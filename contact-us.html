How to get to Hotel JEM (birmingham)

Forms of transportation

Car

If you are driving from the motorway you can get off the M6 at junction 4 and follow the signs for the Alexander Stadium

*we do have a free car park for all of our visitors*

Train

If you are catching the train, the closest train station is on Rocky Lane which is a short train ride from New Street Station in the City Centre

Bus

You can catch the 33, 62 or 107 bus and get off outside Perry Park on Walsall rd and its just a 3 minute walk to us.
